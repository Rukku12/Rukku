Theme of Movie

Bangalore Days is a Comedy-Romantic Entertainer, in which Dulquer Salmaan, Nazriya Nazim, Fahadh Faasil, Nivin Pauly, Nithya Menon, Parvathi Menon, and Isha Talwar play the lead roles. The movie is a fun narrative about three young people who crossover from Kerala to Bangalore to dream, discover, and explore. Nazriya Nazim, Nivin Pauly, and Dulquer Salman are cousins. They individually took different paths after reaching Bangalore to fulfill their dreams. The movie becomes more interesting when Fahadh Fazil joins these cousins. The main crux of the movie is how Bangalore life affects these cousins. Parvathy Menon, Isha Talwar, and Nitya Menon also play important roles in Bangalore Days. Bangalore Days is directed by Anjali Menon and produced by Anwar Rasheed under the banner of Anwar Rasheed Entertainment and Weekend Blockbusters. Cinematography of the movie is handled by Sameer Thahir, whereas Gopi Sunder scores music for the movie.
